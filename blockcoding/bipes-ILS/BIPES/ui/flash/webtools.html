MicroPython Installer

ESP32 & ESP8266

Connect your board via USB, select the board type, and click "Connect" to install MicroPython directly from your browser!

ESP32 Generic: Standard ESP32 boards with 4MB flash
ESP32-S2: Boards with native USB support
Wemos D1 Mini: 4MB QIO flash mode (most Wemos boards)
Generic ESP8266: 4MB DIO mode (NodeMCU, ESP-01)
Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
